About Woody Forestry

Professional, practical support for the full lifecycle of forest ownership.

What we do

We help landowners and timber investors make confident decisions, from planning and inventory to timber sales and regeneration. The goal is simple: improve forest health, reduce risk, and grow long term value.

  • Clear recommendations with realistic budgets and schedules.
  • Better sale outcomes through prep, marketing, and oversight.
  • On the ground support that keeps projects moving.

How it works

  1. Intro call: Goals, timeline, and property basics.
  2. Field visit: Walk the tract, evaluate stands, identify constraints.
  3. Plan or sale package: Maps, recommendations, and next steps.
  4. Execution support: Coordinate vendors, check quality, and document results.

About

Practical forestry that balances revenue, risk, and long term stewardship.

Our approach

Every tract is different. We start by understanding your objectives, then build a plan that is realistic for the property, the market, and the timeline. When it is time to act, we help coordinate the work and document results.

  • Clear deliverables including maps, stand summaries, and next steps.
  • Sale focused execution with attention to market timing and prep.
  • Respect for your land with practical BMP minded recommendations.

How it works

  1. Intro call: Goals, property basics, and timeline.
  2. Site visit: Stand conditions, access, and constraints.
  3. Plan or sale package: Clear scope, maps, and recommendations.
  4. Support through completion: Coordination, oversight, and documentation.

Working with a Forester FAQs

Quick answers that help landowners understand the process.

What is the first step?

Start with a brief call to discuss your goals, then schedule a site visit. From there, we outline a plan or a timber sale approach that fits your tract and timeline.

What are the benefits of hiring a consulting forester?

A good consultant helps you avoid expensive mistakes, improves sale outcomes through preparation and marketing, and creates a clear plan that protects long term value.

How do fees usually work?

Many projects can be hourly or fixed fee. Timber sale work is often a percentage of sale proceeds. We will keep pricing simple and clearly define deliverables up front.

Do you help with reforestation and site prep?

Yes. We can recommend prescriptions, coordinate contractors, and verify establishment so the next rotation gets off to a strong start.
